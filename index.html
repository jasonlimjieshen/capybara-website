GamingBARA started his gaming career since he was 3 months old. He found a gaming setup while he hunts for food. With his hard work coupled with his talent, he succesfully made it to the top in the 2023 Capybara Championship.

He wants to live his life in hard mode and challenge the impossible. 3 years later, he was found standing in the woods unfazed. He even got a cape and a sunglasses to prevent him from the sunlight.
